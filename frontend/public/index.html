Honoring Computing Pioneers

This game celebrates the contributions of:

  • Ada Lovelace (1815-1852) - First Computer Programmer
  • Grace Hopper (1906-1992) - Creator of the First Compiler
  • Alan Turing (1912-1954) - Father of Computer Science
  • Hedy Lamarr (1914-2000) - Inventor of Frequency-Hopping Technology
  • Katherine Johnson (1918-2020) - NASA Mathematician
  • Margaret Hamilton (1936-present) - Apollo Software Engineer
  • Barbara Liskov (1939-present) - Pioneer in Programming Languages
  • Adele Goldberg (1945-present) - Co-Developer of Smalltalk

Mission Statement

SHE<Codes/> aims to make coding feel empowering, inclusive, and creative while celebrating the diverse pioneers who shaped our digital world.

"You've learned from the best. Now it's your turn to write the future."
